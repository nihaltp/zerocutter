🎮 Welcome to Zero Cutter!

Zero Cutter is a multiplayer strategy game.

➡️ On each turn, you can mark one circle.
➡️ If you mark the last circle in any row, column, or diagonal, you earn points.
➡️ The game continues until there are no circles left.
➡️ The player with the highest score at the end wins! 🏆
